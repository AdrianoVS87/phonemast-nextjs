## Introduction

As the demand for better mobile coverage continues to rise, the installation of phone masts on building rooftops has become increasingly common. While these installations are essential for enhancing communication networks, they often bring about unforeseen challenges, particularly regarding roof damage. We understand the complexities and concerns landlords face when dealing with roof repairs caused by Phone Mast installations.

In this blog, we will explore the common issues associated with Phone Mast damage, how we can help streamline roof repairs, and how we assist landlords in recovering compensation and ensuring compliance with lease terms.

## Common Issues with Phone Mast Roof Damage

### Structural Damage:

Phone masts can cause significant structural damage to roofs, leading to leaks, weakened supports, and compromised roof integrity. Heavy equipment, drilling, and constant maintenance activities contribute to wear and tear, making regular inspections and repairs crucial.

### Water Ingress:

Improper installation or maintenance of phone masts can lead to water ingress, which can damage the building’s interior and cause mould growth, electrical issues, and deterioration of building materials.

### Compromised Insulation:

The installation of phone masts may disrupt the roof’s insulation, leading to energy inefficiency and increased heating or cooling costs for the building.

### Health and Safety Hazards:

Damaged roofs can pose health and safety risks to tenants, visitors, and maintenance personnel. It is essential to address these issues promptly to prevent accidents and potential liability.
