### On-Page SEO Per Page
- Title: <60 chars, keyword-first
- Meta description: <160 chars, includes keyword + CTA
- H1: one per page, contains primary keyword
- H2s: contain secondary keywords
- Internal linking: every page links to 3+ related pages
- Image alt text: descriptive, keyword-relevant
- URL structure: clean, keyword-rich slugs

### Content SEO

  - Company reg number in footer
