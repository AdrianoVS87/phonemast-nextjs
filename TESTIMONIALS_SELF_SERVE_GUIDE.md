# Managing Client Testimonials — Self-Serve Guide

Hi Charleen and Raquel,

You can now add, edit, hide, and reorder client testimonials on the homepage yourselves, without needing to email Adriano or wait for a developer release. This guide walks you through it. Should take about two minutes to add a new testimonial.

---

## How to add a new testimonial

1. **Go to the Studio.**
   Open [https://www.phonemastadvice.co.uk/studio](https://www.phonemastadvice.co.uk/studio) in your browser.
   Sign in with your Google or email account (Adriano can grant you access if you haven't been added yet).

2. **Click "Client Testimonial" in the left sidebar.**
   You'll see the existing testimonials listed.

3. **Click the green "Create" button (top right) and choose "Client Testimonial".**

4. **Fill in the fields:**

   | Field | What to put | Required? |
   |---|---|---|
   | **Client name** | The client's name as they want it shown — e.g. "Mr. Robert Kell" or "John & Patricia Hargreaves" | Yes |
   | **Role or client type** | Optional. e.g. "Director", "Rent Review Client". Leave blank if not relevant. | No |
   | **Location or company** | Optional. e.g. "Yorkshire", "Compton Beauchamp Estates". | No |
   | **Quote** | The testimonial itself. **Do not** put quotation marks around it — the website adds them automatically. | Yes |
   | **Rating** | A number from 1 to 5. Defaults to 5. | Yes |
   | **Date received** | The date the testimonial was given to you. Used to sort. | Yes |
   | **Show on website** | Tick to display. Untick to keep the entry but hide it from the homepage. | Yes (defaults to ticked) |
   | **Manual order** | Optional. Lower numbers appear first on the page. Leave blank to sort by date. | No |

5. **Click "Publish" (bottom right).**
   That's it. The website updates within about a minute. Refresh the homepage to see your new testimonial.

---

## How to edit or hide a testimonial

- **Edit:** Click any testimonial in the list, change the fields, click "Publish".
- **Hide it temporarily:** Untick "Show on website" and Publish. The testimonial stays in the system but disappears from the public site. Tick it again to bring it back.
- **Reorder:** Set the "Manual order" field. Lower numbers go first. e.g. order = 1 makes a testimonial the top-left card. Leave blank to let date sort naturally.

## Tips for good testimonial copy

- **Keep quotation marks out** of the quote field — the site styles them automatically. If you paste a quote with `"…"` around it, just delete the marks.
- **Use British English** — e.g. "specialised", "organisation", "favour".
- **Don't break the rules in `CLAUDE.md`** — in particular:
  - Refer to **CTIL** and **Cornerstone** as the same company (one is the legal name, the other is the brand).
  - Refer to the court as the **Upper Tribunal (Lands Chamber)**, never "Upper Land Tribunal".
  - For Compton Beauchamp, the wording is "Matt instigated and advised on the case" — please don't write that Matt "won" or "fought" it.

## Troubleshooting

- **"I published it but I don't see it on the site."** — Wait one minute, then refresh. The homepage caches for 60 seconds. If it still doesn't appear after two minutes, check that "Show on website" is ticked.
- **"I'm getting an authentication error in Studio."** — Sign out and sign back in. If it persists, ping Adriano.
- **"I want to delete a testimonial permanently."** — In Studio, open the testimonial, click the three-dot menu (top right), choose "Delete". Or just untick "Show on website" if you might want it back later.
